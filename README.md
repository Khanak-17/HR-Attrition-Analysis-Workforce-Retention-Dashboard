# 📊 HR Attrition Analysis & Workforce Retention Dashboard

### Python · SQL · Power BI · Excel

This project presents an interactive **HR Analytics Dashboard** developed using **Power BI** to analyze employee attrition, workforce demographics, employee satisfaction, salary distribution, and retention trends.

It enables HR professionals and business managers to identify key attrition factors, monitor workforce KPIs, and make data-driven decisions through interactive visualizations and dynamic filtering.

# 📂 Dataset Overview

The dataset contains **1,470 employee records** with multiple workforce-related attributes across the following categories:

- Attrition Metrics  
- Employee Demographics  
- Salary & Income Information  
- Job Satisfaction Metrics  
- Department & Job Role Details  
- Work-Life Balance Information  
- Performance Ratings

# 📊 Dashboard Features

## 1. 📈 KPI Overview

Interactive KPI cards displaying:

- Total Employees  
- Active Employees  
- Attrition Count  
- Attrition Rate  
- Average Salary  
- Average Age  
- Average Years at Company  

These metrics dynamically update based on selected filters and slicers.

---

## 2. 🏢 Department-wise Attrition Analysis

Analyze employee attrition across major departments:

- Human Resources  
- Research & Development  
- Sales  

Helps identify departments with high employee turnover.

---

## 3. 👨‍💼 Attrition by Job Role

Visualizes attrition trends across multiple job roles including:

- Sales Executive  
- Laboratory Technician  
- Research Scientist  
- Healthcare Representative  
- Manager  

Helps organizations identify high-risk job positions.

---

## 4. 👥 Workforce Demographics

Provides demographic insights including:

- Age Group Distribution  
- Gender Distribution  
- Marital Status  
- Education Background  

Useful for workforce planning and diversity analysis.

---

## 5. 😊 Employee Satisfaction Analysis

Includes analysis of:

- Job Satisfaction  
- Environment Satisfaction  
- Relationship Satisfaction  
- Work-Life Balance  

Helps understand factors influencing employee retention.

---

## 6. 💰 Salary & Income Insights

Analyzes:

- Monthly Income Distribution  
- Salary Slab-wise Attrition  
- Overtime Impact on Attrition  

Helps identify compensation-related attrition patterns.

---

## 7. 🕒 Years at Company Analysis

Tracks employee retention based on tenure and experience levels.

Identifies whether attrition is higher among new employees or experienced professionals.

# 📉 Trends Analysis

## 👨‍💼 Age Group Trends

Employees aged **25–34 years** show comparatively higher attrition rates due to career growth opportunities and work-life balance challenges.

---

## 💼 High Attrition Job Roles

Higher turnover is observed among:

- Laboratory Technicians  
- Sales Executives  
- Research Scientists  

This highlights the need for focused retention strategies.

---

## 😊 Satisfaction & Performance Trends

The dashboard reveals a strong relationship between:

- Low Job Satisfaction  
- Poor Work-Life Balance  
- Overtime Burden  
- Lower Performance Ratings  

and increased employee attrition.

---

# 💡 Key Insights

- Employees aged 26–35 contribute the highest attrition rate  
- Sales department experiences maximum employee turnover  
- Employees with lower salaries are more likely to leave  
- Overtime employees show higher attrition percentages  
- Poor work-life balance strongly impacts retention  
- Job satisfaction significantly affects employee retention

# 🚀 Usage Instructions

1. Open the Power BI dashboard file `HR dashboard.pbix`  
2. Load `HR Data.xlsx` or `HR_Analytics.csv` if required  
3. Navigate through dashboard pages  
4. Apply filters using:
   - Department  
   - Job Role  
   - Age Group  
   - Gender  
   - Salary Slab  
5. Analyze workforce insights and attrition trends
